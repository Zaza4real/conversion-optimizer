## High-value keywords (for product pages, collections, content)

Use these in product titles, descriptions, collection names, and blog content to attract search traffic and buyers:

| Category | Keywords / phrases |
|----------|--------------------|
| **Trust & credibility** | free shipping, easy returns, secure checkout, trusted store, money-back guarantee, customer reviews, verified purchase |
| **Intent / action** | buy online, shop now, order today, free delivery, best price, in stock, fast shipping |
| **Product / category** | [your product type], best [product] for [use], [brand] alternative, [material] [product], sustainable, eco-friendly |
| **Conversion / UX** | mobile-friendly shop, fast checkout, guest checkout, save at checkout, discount code, sale |
| **Long-tail (high intent)** | best [product] under $50, [product] with free returns, where to buy [product] online, [product] for [audience] |

## Features that help your Shopify store sell and be more visible

1. **Strong product pages** — Clear titles, detailed descriptions, multiple images, alt text. Better for SEO and conversion.
2. **Trust signals** — Guarantees, return policy, contact info, reviews. Shown in your Conversion Optimizer recommendations.
3. **Fast, mobile-friendly theme** — Online Store 2.0 theme, optimized images, quick load. Good for Core Web Vitals and mobile search.
4. **Meta title & description** — Every page (home, collections, products) with a unique title (≈50–70 chars) and meta description (≈150–320 chars) that include main keywords.
5. **Structured data** — Use Shopify’s built-in product/org schema so Google can show rich results (price, availability, ratings).
6. **Clear navigation & search** — Easy menus, filters, and search so visitors and crawlers find products and collections.
7. **Blog or content** — Guides, “best of”, how-tos using the keywords above to capture informational and commercial searches.
8. **Secure checkout & policies** — HTTPS, clear refund/shipping pages. Builds trust and supports quality signals.
